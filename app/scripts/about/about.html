About FMSC

Feed My Starving Children is a Christian non-profit orginization founded in 1987 that is dedicated to feeding hungry children in developing nations. They've operated in nearly seventy countries by sending meals, hand-packed by volunteers, to orphanages, schools, clinics, and feeding programs, all in an effort to elimite hunger. For more information on Feed My Starving Children, visit their website, fmsc.org
